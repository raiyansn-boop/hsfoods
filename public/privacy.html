Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how HSFOODS ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our customer ordering app and website, our management app, and our WhatsApp ordering service (together, the "Services"). By using the Services, you agree to this policy.

1.Information we collect

We collect only what we need to take and deliver your orders:

  • Contact details — your WhatsApp / mobile number and your name.
  • Delivery information — the delivery address and any delivery notes or preferred time slot you provide.
  • Order information — the items you order, quantities, amounts, payment method (Cash on Delivery or UPI), and order status.
  • Wallet & referral data — cashback and referral credits earned and redeemed, and any referral code you share or enter.
  • Device & usage data — basic technical information such as app version and general usage needed to keep the Services working.
  • Local storage — we save your cart, phone number, and session on your own device so your basket and login are remembered. This stays on your device.

We do not collect card numbers, bank details, or passwords. UPI payments are completed in your own UPI app, and Cash on Delivery is paid directly to the delivery person — we never see or store your payment credentials.

2.How we use your information

  • To create your account, verify your phone number (one-time code), and log you in.
  • To process, pack, and deliver your orders.
  • To send you order updates (confirmation, packing, shipping, delivery) over WhatsApp or SMS.
  • To calculate and apply wallet cashback and referral rewards.
  • To provide customer support and respond to your requests.
  • To keep the Services secure and prevent fraud or abuse.

3.WhatsApp & messaging

We use the WhatsApp Business Platform (Meta) and/or SMS to send order-related messages and login codes. Your phone number is shared with these messaging providers only to deliver these messages. We do not send marketing messages without your consent, and you can ask us to stop non-essential messages at any time.

4.How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only as needed to run the Services:

  • Delivery — your name, address, and phone number are shared with the delivery person to complete your order.
  • Service providers — messaging (WhatsApp/Meta, SMS), cloud hosting, and similar providers that operate the Services on our behalf.
  • Legal reasons — if required by law, regulation, or valid legal process, or to protect the rights and safety of our users and business.

5.Data retention

We keep your order and account information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Services, resolve disputes, and meet legal or accounting requirements. One-time login codes are short-lived and are deleted shortly after use or expiry.

6.Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information. Passwordless one-time codes reduce the risk of stolen passwords. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data and limit access to it.

7.Your rights

You can ask us to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct or update your details (name, address, phone).
  • Delete your account and associated personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Stop non-essential messages.

To make a request, contact us using the details below.

8.Children's privacy

The Services are intended for users aged 18 and over and are not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

9.Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the Services after changes means you accept the updated policy.
